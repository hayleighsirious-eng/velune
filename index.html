Everything you need

Built for how tutorial centres actually work.

Every feature in Velune was designed around real problems faced by Nigerian tutorial centre owners — not generic software repurposed for education.

01
Student Portal
Each student gets their own login to check fee status, payment history, VIP status and access countdown — without bothering the admin.
02
VIP Access Control
Students who pay in full are automatically granted VIP access with a serial number. Grant or remove temporary access at any time.
03
Cycle Countdowns
Track exactly how many days remain in each student's subscription cycle. Visual timers turn amber and red as expiry approaches.
04
WhatsApp Reminders
Send personalised payment reminders via WhatsApp with one click — pre-filled with the student's name, balance, and due date.
05
Live Alert System
The alerts panel surfaces students whose VIP cycles expire within 5 days, colour-coded by urgency. Never miss a renewal again.
06
CSV Export & Audit Trail
Export your complete student roster or payment history to CSV at any time. Full audit trail with void support for correcting errors.
Simple by design

From sign-up to running
in under 10 minutes.

STEP 01
Create your account
Sign up in seconds with your centre name and email. Choose recurring monthly billing or one-time payment mode.
STEP 02
Add your students
Enter each student's name, class and fee. The system automatically generates login credentials and tracks their balance.
STEP 03
Record payments
Log payments as they come in. Students who pay in full are automatically promoted to VIP with a unique serial number.
STEP 04
Stay in control
Monitor who's paid, who's owing, and who's about to expire — all from your dashboard. Send reminders with one tap.
