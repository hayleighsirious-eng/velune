Frequently Asked Questions

Click the Add Student button at the top right of the dashboard. Fill in the student's full name, class, total fee, initial payment amount, and their WhatsApp number. A login ID or PIN is generated automatically and shown to you after saving. Write it down and share it with the student so they can access their own record.
VIP status is granted automatically when a student completes full payment of their fee. It gives them access to the student portal and activates their serial number. You can also grant Temporary VIP manually from the student's profile if you want to give access before payment is complete — this can be removed at any time.
Once a student's cycle ends and they have fully paid their balance, a Register New Month button appears in their profile. You can also be prompted automatically after recording a final payment. Registering a new month resets their balance to the full fee, clears their serial number, and starts a fresh cycle. The student must pay again to regain VIP access.
When a student's payment cycle is close to expiring, they appear in the Alerts section. If they have a WhatsApp number saved on their profile, clicking the WhatsApp button opens a chat directly to that student with a pre-written reminder message. You just review and send. If no student number is saved, the message opens to your own WhatsApp number as a fallback.
Voiding a payment reverses that amount from the student's balance and removes their VIP status if the reversal causes their balance to go above zero. The payment record remains visible but is struck through and marked as voided. This cannot be undone, so only void a payment if it was recorded in error.
Pausing a cycle freezes the countdown timer for a student — useful if a student takes a break but will return. All their records and payment history are kept. Deleting a student permanently removes them and all their payment records from the system. Deletion cannot be undone.
In the Students section and the Payments section there are export buttons that download your data as a CSV file. This file can be opened in Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet application. It includes all student details, payment history, serial numbers, and cycle information.
